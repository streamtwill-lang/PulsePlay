About Us

PulsePlay is a leading streaming platform located in Nigeria. We combine movies, music, live TV, and series into one seamless experience.

Our mission is to connect people to entertainment they love—anywhere, anytime with high-quality, accessible streaming across all devices.

Founded in 2020, we’ve grown into a trusted name in digital entertainment, dedicated to innovation, accessibility, and top-notch quality.

PulsePlay is committed to providing personalized recommendations, multi-device support, and community engagement features that make entertainment social and fun.

We continuously expand our content library and partner with creators to bring exclusive releases and local favorites to our audience.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: Pushing the boundaries of streaming technology for unmatched user experience.
  • Accessibility: Making entertainment available to everyone, everywhere.
  • Community: Building a vibrant community around shared love for movies, music, and more.
  • Quality: Delivering premium content and smooth performance always.
  • Trust: Prioritizing data security and customer satisfaction.
